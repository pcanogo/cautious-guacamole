How To Make Guacamole

Time to make: 10 mins - Difficulty: Beginner

Save

Ingridients

  • 2 Hass Avocados
  • 1/2 Onion (Of your choice)
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Lime
  • 5g Coriander
  • 1/2tp Salt
  • 1/2tp Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes and onions into small pieces.
  2. Chop up coriander
  3. Use a knife to cut the Avocados in half, cutting through the long side to make sure pieces are even.
  4. Take out the seed then use a spoon to take out the contents of the avocados into a bowl
  5. Using to forks mash the avocados.
  6. Add the tomatoes, onions, salt, pepper, coriander and juice from the Lime
  7. Mix some more
  8. ???
  9. Profit
